>>>About PyCon Africa


The PyCon Africa 2019 conference, which will take place in Accra, is the first pan-African gathering of the Python community on using and developing the Python programming language.

It is produced and run by a multi-national team of volunteers and chaired by a director of the Python Software Foundation. Through Pycon Africa we intend to highlight early adopters of the programming language across the continent, strengthen and advance regional Python communities, and facilitate an enjoyable experience for all in attendance.

The 2019 edition of the conference has a particular emphasis on Data science, Education and Web development.


Schedule


◉ Running Python Communities Workshop Day
Wednesday, 7 August 2019. Our team is inviting community leaders from across the region to attend this one day workshop on how to effectively and sustainably run a Python community. This workshop will be led by Daniele Procida, a member of the Django Project core team and a Director of the Django Software Foundation.
Attendees will learn the essentials of how to run a successful pycon, including how to create a budget, how to lead a team, and how to create a safe environment for attendees.


◉ Conference Days
Thursday, 8 August– Friday 9 August 2019.
See what's going on in the Python world, meet your fellow Pythonistas, share your knowledge and experience, make contacts, brainstorm projects, discuss prospects.



◉ Scheduled talks
Each day begins with a plenary keynote, followed by a schedule that will consist of talks and panels on a wide variety of topics, satisfying a wide range of interests and approaches to programming.



◉ Lightning talks
At the end of each day we dedicate time to a quick series of 5-minute talks on almost any topic. The fast-paced volleys of lightning talks are fired in front of a large audience, making them a very effective way to reach out to the community.



◉ PyLadies Auction and Lunch



◉ Development Sprints
Saturday 10 August 2019.
Help advance your favourite open source project, whether a library, community site, or even the Python language itself!
We provide the space and the infrastructure — network, power, tables, chairs — you provide the enthusiasm and the brainpower. All experience levels welcome.
